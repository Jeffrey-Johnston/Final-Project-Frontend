About Our Team

We are Full-Time Front End Bootcamp students at Grand Circus in Detroit, MI and the creators of CARDio. We created this app because we want to give every person a tool to take their health and liveliness, specifically during this pandemic, into their own hands.

Whether you’ve transitioned from gym to home, or you’re completely new to the exercise world, we think exercise is a key way to maintain your morale.

The big scale goal of this application is to provide structure in a workout for those who aren’t sure where to start, in a fun and interactive way.
