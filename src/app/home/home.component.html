CARDio will get you fired up!

When the world is trying to hold you back.

This pandemic has been a trying time for everybody. Are your energy levels depleted? Exhausted from being confined to your home? Running out of things to do? The team at CARDio believes one of the best ways to combat these situations is to exercise, but where do you start? With CARDio, you can exercise in the comfort of your own home, or take your workout outside.

Workouts for every fitness level

Search through our database of exersises and discover a variety of workouts that can easily be done at home. Filter by exercise name, target muscle group, and level of difficulty. CARDio makes it simple to find what fits you.

Can't find the exercises you're looking for?

Our collection of exercises is forever growing. Users are able to submit workouts of their own that will be stored in our database, and will instantly be available for every user to try. We're all in this together!

Looking for an extra challenge?

Try CARDio's built in card game and race against the clock to complete a series of workouts. Select your favortie exercises or let CARDio generate them for you. Start the game and see if you can make it through the whole deck!

Track Your Stats

After finishing your card game workout you will receive a summary showing what exercises you did, how many cards you completed, and the time that it took. Save your workout stats as a PDF and try and beat your scores on future workouts.
