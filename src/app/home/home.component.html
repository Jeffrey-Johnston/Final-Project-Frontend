Welcome to CARDio

CARDio will get you fired up!

When the world is trying to hold you back!

This pandemic has been a trying time for everybody. Are your energy levels depleted? Exhausted from being confined to your home? Running out of things to do? The team at CARDio believes one of the best ways to combat these situations is to exercise, but where do you start? With CARDio, you can exercise in the comfort of your on home, or take your workout outside.

Workouts for every fitness level

Search through our database of exersises and discover a variety of workouts that can easily be done at home. Filter by workout name, body section and level of difficulty. CARDio makes it simple to find what fits you.

Can't find the exercises that you like?

Our collection of exerises is forever growing. Users are able to submit workouts of there own that will be stored in our database, and instanstly be available for every user to try. We're all in this together!

Looking for an extra challenge?

Try CARDio's built in card game and race against the clock to complete a series of workouts. Select a set of your favortie exercises or let CARDio generate them for you. Start the game and see if you can make it through the whole deck!

Track your progress.

When you finish your workout you will receive a summary of your stats. Save your workout to compare with future workouts and and see if you can beat your best time and number of cards completed.
